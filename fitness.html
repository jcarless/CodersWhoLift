Negative Crunches

Make sure you have put the barbell at a height where you can comfortably take it out and put it back in. Take it out and make yourself ready: The bar is somewhat lower than your shoulders. The feet are quite apart and point out. The head is in your neck and looks up. The chest is out. Go now slowly down, till your thighs are parallel with the floor, not lower. The knees point outwards, your butt, out. Make a small pause of 1 second and with as much energy as you can, push the weight up. Make a pause of 2 seconds and repeat.
